# Suggested SOPs

Date: 2026-06-29

These SOPs are suggestions for customer/company validation planning. They no sustituyen SOPs ni validación regulatoria.

- User access provisioning and deprovisioning.
- Role and account scope review.
- Supplier private commercial data handling.
- Document approval and obsolescence.
- Quote approval and revision.
- Purchase order and shipment traceability review.
- Complaint/NCR intake and escalation.
- CAPA action management and effectiveness checks.
- FMEA review and residual risk acceptance.
- Evidence package creation, approval, export, and retention.
- Audit trail review.
- Backup/restore execution and restore drill evidence.
- Release approval and rollback decision.
- Incident response and customer notification.
