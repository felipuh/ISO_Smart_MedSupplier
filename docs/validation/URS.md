# User Requirements Specification

## Purpose
Support a controlled Supplier-Customer workspace for medical supplier operations, quality evidence, commercial privacy, and audit-ready traceability.

## Requirements
- Users shall access data only within their organization and assigned customer account scope.
- Supplier roles shall access private commercial information only when authorized.
- Customer roles shall never receive Supplier private margin, cost, commission, forecast, or internal notes.
- Quality users shall manage NCR/CAPA/FMEA records with controlled visibility.
- Sensitive approvals and closures shall require reason capture and e-signature.
- Audit and evidence records shall be exportable for validation-supporting review.
