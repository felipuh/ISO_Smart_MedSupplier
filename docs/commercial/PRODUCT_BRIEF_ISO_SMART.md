# Product Brief - ISO Smart

Date: 2026-06-29

## One-Liner

ISO Smart is a modular management platform for ISO-oriented operating systems, governance, risk, objectives, evidence, and continuous improvement workflows.

## Role In The Ecosystem

ISO Smart provides the broader quality and management-system foundation. MedSupplier is a productized vertical workspace that can be sold with or alongside ISO Smart while keeping its own product identity.

## Core Capabilities

- Organization-scoped operational records.
- Context, risk, objectives, process, resource, operations, performance, and improvement modules.
- Authentication and role-aware access.
- Evidence-supporting workflows.
- Reporting and dashboard surfaces.

## Ecosystem Fit

- ISO Smart: broad management-system workspace.
- MedSupplier: supplier/customer regulated operations workspace.
- AdminApps: organizations, users, roles, products, entitlements, billing, and product access authority.

## Positioning

ISO Smart can support controlled demos, paid pilots, proof of concept work, production-like non-regulated deployments, and validation-ready review packages when paired with appropriate SOPs, configuration, and customer/company validation.

## Limits

ISO Smart is not a substitute for customer SOPs, certification audits, regulatory validation, or legal compliance review.
