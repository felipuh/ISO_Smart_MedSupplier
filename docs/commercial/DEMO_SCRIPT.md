# Demo Script - ISO Smart MedSupplier

Date: 2026-06-29

## Goal

Show how MedSupplier supports supplier/customer collaboration while protecting private commercial data and preserving audit-ready evidence.

## Demo Flow

1. Open with ecosystem context:
   - AdminApps controls organizations, users, product access, and billing.
   - MedSupplier owns supplier/customer operational workflows.

2. Login as Supplier Admin:
   - Show account list.
   - Create or review a customer account.
   - Show audit trail creation.

3. Show Document Room:
   - Approve a document with reason.
   - Explain e-signature and audit event.
   - Show obsolescence concept.

4. Show RFQ/Quote:
   - Open a quote.
   - Explain customer-safe view versus supplier-private financial data.
   - Show quote revision concept.

5. Show Orders/Shipments:
   - Open purchase order traceability.
   - Highlight lines, lot, shipment, ASN/ETA/POD, and inspection chain.

6. Show Quality:
   - Open NCR/CAPA.
   - Explain root cause, corrective/preventive action, effectiveness, evidence, and closure reason.

7. Show Evidence Package:
   - Show package index.
   - Show export-ready evidence.

8. Switch to Customer role:
   - Confirm scoped account view.
   - Confirm private cockpit denied.
   - Confirm private commercial fields hidden.

9. Close with readiness language:
   - validation-ready and audit-ready,
   - requires formal validation by customer/company,
   - does not replace SOPs or regulatory validation.

## Avoid During Demo

- Do not claim automatic regulatory compliance.
- Do not use real patient, supplier confidential, or customer production data without written approval.
- Do not promise deployment timelines before scope review.
