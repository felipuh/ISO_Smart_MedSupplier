# Pilot Support Model - ISO Smart MedSupplier

Date: 2026-06-29

## Support Scope

Support applies only to the contracted pilot scope and agreed tenant/users/data.

Included:

- Pilot kickoff support.
- Weekly review meeting.
- Product usage questions.
- Defect triage.
- AdminApps entitlement/billing access triage.
- Role and tenant-scope triage.
- Evidence package review support.
- Final findings summary.

Not included unless separately contracted:

- 24/7 production support.
- Live regulated production incident response.
- Customer SOP ownership.
- Formal validation execution.
- Infrastructure operations outside agreed deployment.
- Third-party integration ownership.

## Channels

- Primary: email or ticket queue.
- Pilot review: scheduled weekly meeting.
- Urgent pilot blocker: named escalation contact during business hours or contracted support window.

## Response Targets

| Severity | Definition | Target |
| --- | --- | --- |
| S1 pilot blocker | Pilot cannot proceed due to login, entitlement, tenant isolation, or critical workflow failure. | Same business day triage. |
| S2 major issue | Important pilot workflow impaired with workaround available. | Next business day triage. |
| S3 question/minor defect | Usability question, documentation question, or minor issue. | Review in weekly cadence or next business day as agreed. |

## Responsibilities

Smart3AI/Product team:

- Application triage.
- Defect analysis.
- Pilot release evidence.
- AdminApps integration guidance.
- Validation package walkthrough if scoped.

Customer/pilot team:

- User approval.
- Data classification.
- SOP and validation ownership.
- Business acceptance.
- Infrastructure approvals if applicable.

## Exit Support

At pilot closeout, support shifts to one of:

- stop,
- extend pilot,
- controlled enterprise MVP support,
- production-control preparation support.
