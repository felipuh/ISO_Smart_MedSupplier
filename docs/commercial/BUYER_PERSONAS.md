# Buyer Personas

Date: 2026-06-29

## Supplier Quality Director

Needs controlled evidence, NCR/CAPA discipline, FMEA traceability, and customer-ready audit packages.

## Supplier Commercial / Sales Leader

Needs RFQ/quote control, forecast visibility, margin privacy, commission/advance tracking, and a controlled customer workspace.

## Customer Supplier Quality Engineer

Needs scoped access to supplier evidence, quality events, CAPA status, shipment traceability, and audit-ready exports without seeing supplier-private data.

## Operations / Logistics Manager

Needs order, lot, shipment, ASN, ETA, POD, and inspection traceability.

## CFO / Billing Owner

Needs product access tied to entitlements, subscription state, and billing authority through AdminApps.

## IT / Security Reviewer

Needs tenant isolation, role matrix, security headers, CI/CD, backup/restore, rollback, and validation package evidence.

## Quality / Compliance Reviewer

Needs requirements, traceability matrix, test evidence, known issues, SOP impact, and human approval workflow.
