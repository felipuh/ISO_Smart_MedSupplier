# Sales FAQ

Date: 2026-06-29

## Is MedSupplier ready to sell?

It is ready for controlled demos, paid pilots, proof of concept work, and controlled enterprise MVP discussions. Production-like non-regulated deployment can be discussed after scope, environment, support, and approval review.

## Is it validated?

No. It is validation-ready and audit-ready for customer/company review. Formal validation must be executed and approved by the responsible organization.

## Does it replace SOPs?

No. It no sustituye SOPs ni validación regulatoria.

## Can MedSupplier be sold without ISO Smart?

Yes, MedSupplier can be sold as a standalone product if AdminApps remains the authority for organizations, users, entitlements, product access, and billing.

## What makes it valuable for medical supplier workflows?

It connects customer accounts, documents, quotes, purchase orders, shipments, NCR/CAPA, FMEA, evidence packages, roles, audit trail, and supplier-private commercial views in one controlled workspace.

## Can customers see supplier margin?

No. Customer roles are scoped and private commercial fields are filtered server-side.

## What does AdminApps do?

AdminApps controls product access, organizations, users, roles, subscriptions, billing state, and suspension.

## What are the main known limitations?

Binary PDF export is not implemented, restore drill evidence is pending, and formal customer/company validation is pending.

## What is the best first commercial offer?

A paid pilot with clear success criteria, approved data scope, AdminApps entitlement setup, and a validation-ready evidence review.
