# Product Brief - ISO Smart MedSupplier

Date: 2026-06-29

## One-Liner

ISO Smart MedSupplier is a controlled Supplier-Customer workspace for regulated supplier operations, commercial privacy, quality evidence, traceability, and audit-ready collaboration.

## Ideal Use

- Medical device supplier/customer collaboration.
- Supplier quality and logistics control tower.
- RFQ/quote, order, shipment, NCR/CAPA, FMEA, and evidence package workflows.
- Controlled demo, paid pilot, proof of concept, or controlled enterprise MVP.
- Production-like non-regulated deployment after customer/company review.

## Core Capabilities

- Supplier and customer role model.
- Account-scoped customer access.
- Supplier private cockpit for authorized commercial roles.
- Quote revision and private margin controls.
- Purchase order and shipment traceability.
- NCR, CAPA, CAPA actions, and FMEA risk tracking.
- Evidence package index and export.
- Audit trail and e-signature records for critical actions.
- AdminApps product access and billing authority integration.

## Value Proposition

MedSupplier helps suppliers and customers work from one controlled workspace instead of scattered spreadsheets, email chains, and disconnected evidence folders. It is designed to support validation-ready and audit-ready operations while keeping supplier-private commercial data protected.

## Positioning

Allowed positioning:

- controlled demo
- paid pilot
- proof of concept
- controlled enterprise MVP
- production-like non-regulated deployment
- validation-ready system for formal customer/company validation

## Limits

MedSupplier requires formal validation by the customer/company before regulated production use. It does not replace SOPs, training, infrastructure qualification, supplier quality procedures, or regulatory validation.
