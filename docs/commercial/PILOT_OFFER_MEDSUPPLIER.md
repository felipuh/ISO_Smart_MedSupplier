# Pilot Offer - ISO Smart MedSupplier

Date: 2026-06-29

## Offer

ISO Smart MedSupplier paid pilot is a controlled, time-boxed engagement to validate supplier/customer workflows, commercial privacy, quality evidence, traceability, and AdminApps-governed product access with a limited tenant and agreed success criteria.

## Approved Commercial Positioning

MedSupplier may be sold as:

- controlled demo,
- paid pilot,
- proof of concept,
- controlled enterprise MVP,
- validation-ready and audit-ready review package support.

MedSupplier must not be sold as:

- full regulated production,
- formally validated system,
- production-controlled live operation before target gates,
- guaranteed regulatory acceptance,
- broad ecosystem release readiness across ISO Smart, MedSupplier, and AdminApps.

## Pilot Package

Included:

- Pilot kickoff and scope review.
- AdminApps tenant/product entitlement setup guidance.
- MedSupplier demo or approved pilot tenant setup.
- Role configuration for supplier and customer personas.
- Workflow walkthrough for accounts, documents, RFQ/quote, purchase orders, shipments, quality events, CAPA, FMEA, and evidence packages.
- Weekly pilot review.
- Defect triage within contracted scope.
- Validation package walkthrough.
- Final pilot findings summary.

Not included unless separately contracted:

- Formal customer/company validation execution.
- SOP authorship or approval ownership.
- Regulated production go-live.
- Legacy data migration.
- ERP, QMS, EDI, or identity integrations beyond scoped pilot.
- Infrastructure ownership.
- Legal or regulatory opinion.

## Entry Criteria

- Signed pilot scope and limitations.
- Named pilot owner and support contact.
- Tenant data classification approved.
- AdminApps organization, entitlement, subscription/billing state configured.
- E2E smoke run before kickoff.
- Known limitations disclosed.

## Exit Criteria

- Success criteria reviewed.
- Pilot limitations and open risks documented.
- Buyer decision recorded: stop, extend pilot, controlled MVP, or prepare production-control gates.

## Decision Boundary

This pilot supports a commercial buying decision. It does not approve regulated production or live controlled production.
