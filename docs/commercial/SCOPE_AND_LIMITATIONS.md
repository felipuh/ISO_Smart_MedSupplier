# Scope And Limitations

Date: 2026-06-29

## In Scope

- Controlled demo.
- Paid pilot.
- Proof of concept.
- Controlled enterprise MVP.
- Production-like non-regulated deployment.
- Validation-ready documentation package.
- Supplier/customer role and account scoping.
- AdminApps product access authority.
- Evidence package support.
- CI/CD and production-like hardening.

## Out Of Scope Unless Separately Contracted

- Formal customer validation execution.
- SOP writing and approval ownership.
- Regulatory submission support.
- Legal compliance opinion.
- Production infrastructure ownership.
- Data migration from legacy systems.
- Binary PDF evidence rendering.
- ERP/QMS/EDI integrations beyond scoped contract.

## Required Before Regulated Production Use

- Formal validation protocol and execution.
- Human approval signatures.
- SOP alignment.
- Training records.
- Restore drill evidence.
- Deployment environment qualification.

## Plain Boundary

The product can support validation-ready and audit-ready workflows, but it does not replace the customer/company quality system.
