# SLA Draft

Date: 2026-06-29

This is a draft for commercial discussion, not an executed SLA.

## Support Tiers

| Tier | Target Use | Response Target |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Demo | Controlled walkthrough | Best effort |
| Pilot | Paid pilot / POC | 1 business day for high priority |
| Enterprise MVP | Controlled enterprise MVP | 4 business hours for high priority |
| Production-Like Non-Regulated | Approved non-regulated production-like use | 2 business hours for high priority |

## Severity Draft

| Severity | Description |
| --- | --- |
| Critical | Service unavailable or major data access/security issue. |
| High | Critical workflow blocked without workaround. |
| Medium | Workflow impaired with workaround. |
| Low | Cosmetic issue, question, or enhancement. |

## Exclusions

- Customer network, identity provider, or infrastructure failures outside agreed scope.
- Unapproved custom changes.
- Regulatory validation ownership.
- Third-party system outages unless covered by contract.
